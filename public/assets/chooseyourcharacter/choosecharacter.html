Choose Your Character

Mage

Wise mages make careful use of their spells to keep foes at a distance. Mages can summon burst of fire to incinerate distant targets and their surroundings. Despite their powerful offensive spells, they are very fragile and have light armor making them vulnerable to close-range attacks.

​

Knight

Combined with strength and leadership. Known to protect from the front lines with shields, locking down enemies. Knights use combact tactics and different variety of weapons to destroy their enemies. Unlessing their rage at the closest threat. Beware of the knights, they are fearless warriors.

Archer

These type of warriors uses crossbow to attack their foes from a distance. The archer's specialty is range attack and they will do anything to maintain their distance. Do not estimate the power of an archer, the ability to launch their deadly rain of missiles will be enough to kill a whole army.

Rogue

These mercenaries rely on brutal and efficient tactics. Assassins and mastering in stealth, they will approach their enemies from behind with them knowing. Piercing their organs and vanishing into the shadows. They are known for surprise attacks, leading with combat combos.
